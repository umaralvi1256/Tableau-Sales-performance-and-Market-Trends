# Sales Performance & Market Trends Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on visualizing sales performance and market trends to provide a clearer picture of regional profitability and growth patterns. The dashboard was developed to help stakeholders quickly identify top-performing products and sales regions, allowing for more strategic resource allocation.

## Key Features
* **Regional Performance Map:** A geographical visualization of sales distribution to identify high-potential growth regions.
* **Product Trend Analysis:** A time-series analysis of sales volume, helping to track product life cycles and seasonal demand.
* **Interactive Filters:** Allows users to drill down into specific product categories or time periods for deeper investigation.

## Business Value
By visualizing sales data, the dashboard makes it easier for the leadership team to spot market opportunities, optimize product inventory, and monitor regional sales health, ultimately supporting a more proactive approach to sales management.

## Tools Used
* **Tableau:** Used for interactive charting and dashboard layout.
* **Microsoft Excel:** Used for consolidating sales records and performing pre-visualization data modeling.
